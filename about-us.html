About us


The companys history

Pallmaster AB is a small family business that was started by Owe Zackrisson in 1989. At that time, the business was conducted in a premises of 250 m2 and had only one customer.


The company today

Since March 2002, we have been located in Björröd's industrial area in Landvetter, where we have a plot of 14,000 m2 of which premises of 7,000 m2 are adapted to our business. Now the family business is run by Stefan and Johan Zackrisson, and is today one of the largest players in Sweden within the industry.
