Our service

Repair of EUR pallets

We are licensed by SIS/DNV and this gives us permission to carry out repairs of EUR pallets. This also means that our repairs are checked by DNV, all because maintain the high standard we are known for.

Special manufacturing

We manufactures all types of wooden packaging. But export boxes in all formats are our specialty. If you have something that needs packaging, standard size or special, don't hesitate to contact us. We are licensed by the Swedish Board of Agriculture for the manufacture of heat-treated packaging for export to all corners of the world, for example China, Australia and the USA. Our IPPC license is SE-O-3755. The license means that we heat the wood to 56 degrees for 30 minutes. The license guarantees that the wood does not contains vermin and that pests cannot spread over the world.

Sorting

More and more companies hire us to sort pallets properly to pallet depot. Since we are constantly updated on the appli- the regulations, this guarantees a correct result. We also train personnel from various companies in sorting pallets, according to DNV / SIS standards.

Pallet Depot

We operate pallet depots for various companies. We unload, sort, stores and loads on the customer's requests. We also operate a pallet depot for CHEP, the blue pallet, and the flow of these is approximately 25,000 pallets/month.
